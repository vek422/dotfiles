Our values

We ship fast and learn faster. Every day without a release is a day we delay our feedback loop. Speed is our advantage—and our weapon.

We don't build for suits. We build for hackers, engineers, solopreneurs, and product-led teams. Our entire company—from docs to dashboards—must feel like it was built by developers, for developers.

We will never hide behind black boxes. Not in pricing, not in performance, not in policy. Users should always know exactly what they're getting, what they're paying for, and how it works.
Scale the Network
Our ecosystem becomes smarter with every new workflow, integration, and use case. We think in terms of network effects, compounding usage, and creator flywheels—not vanity metrics.
Ownership is the Culture
Everyone at TexAu is a founder in attitude. We own outcomes, act like it's our money, and don't wait for permission to make something better.
